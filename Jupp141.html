2nd Lt. George S. Jupp (1842-1864) [Section 92 Lot 75]

113th NY Vol. Infantry (converted to 7th NY Vol Heavy Artillery)

Enlisted as private, promoted to sergeant, then to first sergeant. Captured at Petersburg, Va.; died of disease, October 31, 1864, at Andersonville, Ga.
