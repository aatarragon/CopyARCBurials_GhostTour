Cpl. Henry L. Dempsey (1840-1914) [Section 16 Lot 60]

125th NY Vol. Infantry:

Enlisted in August 1862 at Troy and mustered in as private. While on picket duty with other members of his regiment at Harper’s Ferry, Va. on September 15, 1862, his regiment left camp without calling in the pickets. Dempsey was captured and paroled the next day. Promoted to corporal in 1863, he was again captured on picket duty in December 1863 at Mine Run, Va.. He was once more paroled and discharged in May 1865. He was the brother of James and John Dempsey. All three brothers are interred in the same plot.
