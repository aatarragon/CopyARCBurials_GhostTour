John Tayler (1742-1829) [Sec 19 Lot 15]

Fifth Governor of New York, Lieutenant Governor of New York under DeWitt Clinton when the bill authorizing the Erie Canal was passed, Assemblyman, NY Senator, one of the first trustees of the newly formed NY State Library in Albany, Chancellor of the State University of NY, The fateful derogatory comments uttered by Alexander Hamilton that led to his duel with Aaron Burr were spoken at Tayler’s home at a dinner attended by Hamilton and his father-in-law Philip Schuyler, Veteran of the Revolutionary War.
