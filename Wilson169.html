Col. John Wilson (1838-1864) [Section 62 Lot 23]

43rd NY Vol. Infantry

Graduated from Albany Academy, he was a florist before the war. He was mustered in as Captain. He was engaged at Lee’s Mills, Warwick Creek, Yorktown, Golden’s Farm, Seven Days’, Antietam, Fredericksburg, Marye’s Heights, Salem Church, Bank’s Ford, Gettysburg, Rappahannock Station, Locust Grove and Mine Run, being promoted up the ranks to Colonel. At the Battle of the Wilderness he was killed in action, May 6, 1864.
