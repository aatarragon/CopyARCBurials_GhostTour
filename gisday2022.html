This 2022 GIS Day event at UAlbany is organized by UAlbany's Department of Geography & Planning, Geography & Planning Student Association, and Center for Undergraduate Research and Creative Engagement.

This in-person envent includes organization and student exhibition , presentations of featured speakers , career panel , and Resume Clinic . Please check out the schedule below and pre-register for attending this event.

All registered attendants will receive free GIS E-books and enter the drawing to receive one of five ArcGIS personal licenses ($100 value each), one year of premium subsciprtion to ArcGIS Story Map, or one of many customized prizes ($10 value each) sponsored by Department of Geography and Planning and Center for Undergraudate Research and Creative Engagement of University at Albany.
