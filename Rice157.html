Sgt. Alexander Davidson Rice (1837-1864) [Section 6 Lot 3]

113th NY Vol. Infantry (7th NY Vol. Heavy Artillery)

Rice enlisted as a private, promoted to corporal and sergeant. He participated in the battles of Spottsylvania, North Anna and Totopotomy Creek. He was mortally wounded in action, June 3, 1864, at Cold Harbor, Va. His body laid on the field all day and was retrieved at night. He died of his wounds, June 28, 1864, at Washington, DC. He was survived by a wife and 2 children.
