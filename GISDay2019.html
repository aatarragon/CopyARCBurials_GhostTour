GIS Day @ UAlbany Seeing the World through GIS

  • November 13, 2019 Event Schedule

12:30 pm – 2:30 pm “Lunch and Learn”

Lab tours of GIS and Drones

Dr. Alexander Buyantuev, Associate Professor, will highlight some of the past and present work he has been doing incorporating drones in Arts and Sciences B05.

Dr. Shiguo Jiang, Assistant Professor, will talk some of his work on Shifting Cultivation and Maya Forest change as well work on Urban crime in Arts and Sciences B03.

Levi Anderson, Elizabeth Hayes, and William Barnes, MSGIS students, will demonstrate using python programming in GIS and allow students to learn python codes in Arts and Sciences B03.

Dr. Rui Li, Assistant Professor, will give a demonstration on Tunnel Orienter, an alternative to Google Maps; and The Albany Rural Cemetery Explorer with adjunct professor Kurt Swartz, an ongoing partnership with the department and Albany Rural Cemetery thanks to a grant from the Bender Family Foundation in Arts and Sciences B04.

3:00 pm – 6:00 pm “GIS in the Captial Region”

Exhibition, presentation, panel, and networking

Organizations: Building FootprintUSA, Capital District Regional Planning Commission, Esri, MJ Engineering, New York State Department of Transportation, New York State Department of State, New York State Department of Environment Conservation, New York State Department of Health, United State Geographical Survey, University at Albany Department of Geography and Planning

3:30 pm – 4:00 pm “Presentation: Beyond GIS”

Dr. Catherine T. Lawson, Associate Professor, Director of the Master in Urban & Regional Planning Program, Affiliated Faculty member of the Department of Informatics in the College of Computing and Information, and the Director of the Lewis Mumford Center/Albany Visualization and Informatics Lab (AVAIL).

4:00 pm – 5:00 pm “Panel: GIS in Career”

Join the conversations with the following panelists about implementation of GIS in everyday life and launching a career with GIS:

  • NYS GIS Clearinghouse
  • MJ Engineering and Land Surveying
  • NYS Department of Transportation
  • NYS Department of Health
  • NYS DEC, Division of Lands and Forests
  • Dr. James Mower, MSGIS Chair and internship coordinator

    • 5:00 pm – 6:00 pm “Keynotes: GIS and Major Events”

    5:00 pm - 5:30 pm - MJ Engineering, a local surveying company, will discuss how they incorporate GIS and drones in their work.

    5:30 pm - 6:00 pm - NYS GIS ClearingHouse, a statewide repository for data, will discuss how their data was used during and after Superstorm Sandy in 2012
