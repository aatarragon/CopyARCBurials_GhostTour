Samuel Stringer (1735-1817) [Sec 40 Lot 9]

Albany’s most prominent physician in the 1700’s, appointed(1755) to the medical unit of the Colonial Army, headed the contiental medical corps during the Revolutionary War, appointed commissioner to examine physicians and surgeons in the city of Albany.
