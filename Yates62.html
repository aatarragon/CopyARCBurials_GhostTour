Robert Yates, (1738-1801) [Sec 8, lot 1]

Revolutionary Patriot, Member of Albany’s Committee of Safety. Supervised the construction of the “Chain across the Hudson River, received a message from Ethan Allen on 5/12/1776 notifying them that he had captured Ticonderoga, Member of the NY Provincial Congress – received a copy of the Declaration of Independence and sent it on to Albany. Member of the Committee that drafted NY’s first constitution, Chief Judge of the NY State Supreme Court.
