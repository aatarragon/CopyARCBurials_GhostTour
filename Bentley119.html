Bvt. Brig. Gen. Richard Charles Bentley (1830-1871) [Section 65 Lot 6]

63rd NY Vol. Infantry.

Charles Bentley was born in Columbia County. He enlisted in the 30th NY Infantry, a Troy regiment. In February 1862 he was discharged for promotion to Major with the 63rd NY Infantry, part of the famous Irish Brigade. Charles was wounded at the Battles of Antietam and Gettysburg. Bentley was one of six men to be honored with the first watch over President Lincoln’s coffin as he lay in state in the State Capitol building in Albany.
