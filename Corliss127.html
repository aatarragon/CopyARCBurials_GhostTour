1st Lt. Stephen Potter Corliss (1842-1904) [Section 18 Lot 74]

Medal of Honor Recipient, 11th NY Vol. Light Artillery (consolidated with 4th NY Vol. Heavy Artillery)

Captured at Reams Station, Va. and exchanged. Placed on detached service to the staff of Maj. Gen. Nelson H. Miles. Awarded Medal of Honor for actions at South Side Railroad, Va. Medal of Honor Citation reads in part, “picked up the flag from a fallen soldier and led an attack against a battery that was inflicting heavy losses on the army.” He was the brother of Pvt. Roswell B. Corliss, Jr.
