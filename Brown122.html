Sgt. Reed L. Brown (1841-1865) [Section 74 Lot 27]

11th NY Vol. Light Artillery (consolidated with 4th NY Vol. Heavy Artillery)

Captured at Ream’s Station, Va. and held in Libby Prison, Richmond, Va. until paroled, Feb 5, 1865, at Aiken's Landing, Va.; mustered out June 28, 1865, at Albany, NY. Died on Christmas Day 1865 in Albany.
