Quartermaster Sergt. Andrew B. Jones (1840-1909) [Section 107 Lot 115]

24th NYS National Guard

Enlisted in 24th Regiment, NYS National Guard. Served as quartermaster-sergeant and quartermaster of the regiment. After the Civil War he became a partner in Hudson Valley Paper Company, wholesale paper dealers.
