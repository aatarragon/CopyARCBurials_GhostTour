Maj. George Seward Dawson (1838-1864) [Section 33 Lot 1]

10th NYS Militia; 2nd NY Vol. Heavy Artillery

Son of George Dawson, editor of Albany Evening Journal. Wounded in action at Petersburg, Va., and died at United States General Hospital (“Armory Square Hospital”) at Albany, December 6, 1864. A posthumous commission to brevet lieutenant colonel was conferred upon Dawson by NYS Gov. Reuben Fenton for gallant and meritorious conduct. Grand Army of the Republic (GAR) Post 63 was named for George S. Dawson.
