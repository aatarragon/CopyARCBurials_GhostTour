Landsman Robert H. King (1845-1865) [Section 18 Lot 15]

Medal of Honor Recipient, United States Navy

Served aboard the experimental ship “Picket Boat No. 1”. King was awarded the Medal of Honor posthumously (1895) for his part in volunteering in the successful sinking of the Confederate ram CSS Albemarle in October 1864. Captured by Rebels and held as Prisoner of War, he was exchanged and died 10 days after returning to Albany.
