1st Lt. John M. Dempsey (1836-1865) [Section 16 Lot 60]

Albany Burgesses Corps; 18th NY Vol. Infantry; 43rd NY Vol. Infantry

After service in the militia, Dempsey served two years in the 18th NY Infantry. Returning to Albany, he re-enlisted in 43rd NY Infantry. He was taken prisoner at the Battle of the Wilderness in 1864 and sent to Libby Prison. He was later released. Dempsey was wounded in the attack on Ft. Fisher, March 25, 1865, and died of his wounds on May 6, 1865, in a hospital at City Point, Va. He was the brother of James and Henry Dempsey. All three brothers are interred in the same plot
