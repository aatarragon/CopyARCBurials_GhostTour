Capt. William T. Wooley (1827-1861) [Section 92 Lot 116]

Albany Republican Artillery, 25th NYS Militia (Albany Regiment)

Wooley was a Captain of Co. A in the Albany Republican Artillery of the 25th NYS Militia. He had joined the army in poor health, ignored the advice of his friends but was committed to restoring the Union. He died in Georgetown, Washington, DC, on July 13, 1861.
