This 2021 GIS Day event at UAlbany is organized by UAlbany's Department of Geography & Planning, Geography & Planning Student Association, and School of Public Health.

This virutal envent includes speaker series and a career panel . Please check out the featured speakers below and sign up for attending individual sessions.

All registered attendants will receive free GIS E-books and enter the drawing to receive one of five ArcGIS personal licenses ($100 value each) or one of many customized prizes ($10 value each) sponsored by Department of Geography and Planning , University at Albany.
