Lt. Alfred Birch Huested, MD (1841-1918) [Section 3 Lot 59]

113th NY Vol. Infantry (7th NY Vol. Heavy Artillery); 21st NY Vol. Cavalry

Graduated from Albany Academy. Became a medical student after meeting Dr. James H. Armsby, a distinguished surgeon and founder of the Albany Medical College. Joined the 113th NY Infantry as hospital steward. Returned to Albany, graduated from Albany Medical College and was detailed to the 21st NY Volunteer Cavalry as Assistant Surgeon. After Civil War, became charter member of the NYS Pharmaceutical Assoc. He was a member of Masonic Temple Lodge, No. 14, F. & A.M. He was also a member of the Military Order of the Loyal Legion of the United States (MOLLUS), and of the Major George S. Dawson Post #63, G.A.R. (Grand Army of the Republic).
