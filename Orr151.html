1st Lt. William Emmet Orr (1841-1864) [Section 56 Lot 15]

113th NY Vol. Infantry (became 7th NY Vol. Heavy Artillery)

A promising lawyer in civilian life, Orr participated in battles of Po River, Milford Station, North Anna River. He was mortally wounded, shot in the shoulder, at North Anna River, May 31, 1864, and was transported to Washington DC for hospital care. He died on June 2, 1864.
