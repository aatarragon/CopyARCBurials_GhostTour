1st Lt. James L. Dempsey (1827-1864) [Section 16 Lot 60]

34th Massachusetts Vol. Infantry

Performed garrison duty around Washington and built fortifications around Ft. Lyon. Engaged in fighting near Harper’s Ferry, Spottsylvania, Winchester (Opequon) and Fisher’s Hill. He was wounded at Cedar Creek, October 1864, struck in the forehead by a bursting shell and died from his wounds. He was the brother of John M. and Henry Dempsey. All three brothers are interred in the same plot.
