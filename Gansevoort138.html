Bvt. Brig. Gen. Henry Sanford Gansevoort (1834-1871) [Section 55 Lot 2]

5th U.S. Light Artillery; 13th NY Vol. Cavalry

Enlisted in the 5th U.S. Artillery as Second Lieutenant, promoted to Captain. Engaged at Battle of Antietam. Later, he enlisted in 13th NY Cavalry as lieutenant colonel and promoted to colonel in 1864. Promoted to brevet brigadier general, June 24, 1865, for faithful and meritorious services. Although he was appointed and nominated, the promotion was not acted upon by U.S. Senate.
